Bit About Us

Welcome to our PG and Tiffin Services! We are a team of dedicated professionals who understand the needs of students, working professionals, and anyone else who's looking for a comfortable, safe, and affordable accommodation with delicious home-cooked food.

Paying Guest Services

Our PG services provide comfortable, fully-furnished rooms with all the necessary amenities like Wi-Fi, TV, AC, refrigerator, washing machine, and more. We ensure that our PGs are located in safe and convenient locations, close to public transportation, markets, and other essential services.

Tiffin Services

We also offer Tiffin services, providing tasty and healthy home-cooked meals delivered right to your doorstep. Our Tiffin services are perfect for those who are too busy to cook or want to enjoy a variety of delicious dishes without the hassle of cooking..

Library Booking

Our book library offers a wide range of books, from bestsellers to classics to niche genres. . Our book library is available for monthly and yearly subscriptions, and customers can easily book their books online through our website.Our online book library booking service allows customers to borrow books online and have them delivered to their doorstep.


PG Services

Our PG services provide comfortable and fully furnished accommodations for students, young professionals, and anyone who needs a temporary place to stay. We offer shared and private accommodations with basic amenities such as a bed, wardrobe, and in some cases, even meals. Our PGs are located in residential areas to provide a homely atmosphere and easy access to nearby facilities.

Tiffin Services

Our tiffin services offer a variety of homemade food options, prepared by experienced chefs. We use fresh ingredients and follow high standards of hygiene to ensure that our customers get healthy and delicious meals. Our tiffin services are available for daily, weekly, and monthly subscriptions, and we offer vegetarian and non-vegetarian options to cater to different dietary preferences.
